How TEE verification works

Every answer Kryos gives is run inside a Trusted Execution Environment on OpenGradient infrastructure — a secure enclave where neither the hosting provider nor anyone else can tamper with the computation.

After each answer, a cryptographic proof of execution is posted on-chain. You can click ◆ TEE verified under any response to open its transaction on OpenGradient explorer and independently confirm:

  • The exact model that ran
  • The exact input prompt
  • The exact output returned to you
  • Timestamp & attestation signature

This means you don't have to trust our server, our prompts, or our logs — you can verify what the model actually said, cryptographically, for every single answer.
